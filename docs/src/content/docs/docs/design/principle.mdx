Supakone.dev is the all in one personal website of Supakone Kongprapan (a.k.a. Prame). It serves as a Information, blog, and resource hub for almost everything related to Supakone.

## Why Supakone.dev?

Supakone.dev is designed to be a comprehensive platform that showcases Supakone's skills, projects, and interests. It provides visitors with insights into his professional journey, personal interests, and aspirations for making a positive impact in the world.

Prame himself want to share his journey to become someone better every day. He believe that by documenting his experiences and sharing his knowledge, he can inspire others to pursue their own goals and make a difference in their lifes. So he willingly open up about his life, work, and thoughts through this website.

<Aside></Aside>
## Core Principles

There are 3 core principles behind Supakone.dev include:
- **Transparency**: Prame believes in being open and honest about his journey, sharing both successes and failures to provide a realistic view of personal and professional growth.
- **No one cares**: Prame understands that, ultimately, people are focused on their own lives and challenges. This realization drives him to create content that is genuine.
- **Keep Sensitive Information Private**: Prame is committed to protecting the privacy of himself and others. He avoids sharing sensitive information that could harm himself or those around him.
